Pixel Brush

Learn to draw pixel art for free, with Pixel Brush.
Our friendly community will help you improve your skills.
Pixel Brush is used by over 100,000 artists around the world.

New to pixel art?

If you're an aspiring artist, or just want to have fun being creative, then pixel art is the medium for you.
Make pictures with a mosaic of colored dots called pixels, and let Pixel Brush help you bring your art to life.

Learn art, not an interface

Tap to draw, pinch to zoom, auto-save, auto-preview. You're here to do art, so Pixel Brush tries to get out of your way.

Premade color palettes

Start your art with a premade color palette, and create your own as you go.

Tools of the trade

Draw pixel perfect shapes, fill entire areas, select blobs of pixels, and more.

Supportive community

The Pixel Brush Discord community is friendly and can provide feedback to help you improve.

Layers to organize your art

Each layer has its own pixels, so you can keep your drawing organized. Rearrange, merge and hide layers as you need.

Coming soon: Animation

Use multiple frames together to animate characters, scenery and more. Then export these as GIFs to share with others.

Get started with drawing with any of the inbuilt palettes, or create your own.

Step-by-step guides to teach you new skills and techniques.
